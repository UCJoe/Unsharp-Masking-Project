To run:
Open ImageEnhancementUI.m in matlab and run.
or
Right click on ImageEnhancementUI.m and press run.

To use UI:
Click the "select file" button to select a jpg.
Move the "Filter Size" slider to increase or decrease the lowpass filter size.
Move the "k" slider to increase or decrease the amount of unsharp mask that is added to the output image.


Other notes:
Best if used for images with height and length between 128px and 512px.
Larger images can be processed but take longer.
